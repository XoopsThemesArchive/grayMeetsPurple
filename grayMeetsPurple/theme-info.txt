Theme 'GrayMeetsPurple' info:

Theme includes,
	-Logo template (PSD and CPT file)
	-Topic image template (PSD and CPT file)
	-New Download and Weblinks logo
	-Template file (tar.gz)
	
Installation,
	-Install it just like any other theme! 
	Copy the logo-en.gif from the extras directory into the '/modules/mydownloads/images/' directory,
	the logo.gif from the extras directory into the '/modules/mylinks/images/' directory.
	
	The print.gif and friend.gif from the extras directory goes into the '/modules/news/images/' 
	directory. That's it!


We hope you will enjoy this theme.
